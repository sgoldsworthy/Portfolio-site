BetMGM Expansion

Delivered analytics for BetMGM's US market expansion, building measurement frameworks for new state launches and cross-market performance benchmarking.

SQL Snowflake Python Power BI Market Analytics

Business Problem

BetMGM was expanding rapidly across US states, each with different regulatory environments, competitive landscapes, and customer demographics. The business needed a consistent analytical framework to measure launch performance, benchmark across markets, and allocate investment efficiently.

Why It Was Difficult

  • Each state had unique regulatory constraints affecting product availability and marketing
  • Market maturity varied dramatically — some states were new, others established
  • No historical baseline for new state launches made forecasting unreliable
  • Competitive intelligence was fragmented and inconsistent

My Role

I built the market analytics framework for the US expansion, including launch measurement templates, cross-market benchmarking, and investment allocation modelling. I worked closely with the US commercial team and UK-based strategy leads.

Approach

Launch Measurement

  • Designed standardised launch scorecards applicable to any new state
  • Defined early-signal metrics that predict long-term market health
  • Built automated reporting for the first 30/60/90 days post-launch

Cross-Market Benchmarking

  • Created normalised comparison frameworks accounting for market size, competition, and regulatory environment
  • Built cohort-adjusted benchmarks so new markets could be compared fairly against mature ones
  • Identified leading indicators of market success vs. underperformance

Investment Allocation

  • Modelled customer acquisition cost and lifetime value by state
  • Built scenario models for different investment levels
  • Quantified diminishing returns to help optimise marketing spend

Architecture

State-Level Data (regulatory, competitive, financial)
      ↓
Normalisation Layer (market size, maturity adjustment)
      ↓
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Launch Scorecards  │  Benchmarking  │
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
      ↓
Investment Models
      ↓
US Leadership & UK Strategy

Technologies

  • SQL — Complex multi-market queries and cohort construction
  • Snowflake — Central data platform spanning UK and US data
  • Python — Market modelling and scenario analysis
  • Power BI — Executive dashboards for cross-market visibility

Outcome

  • Standardised measurement framework adopted across all new state launches
  • Clear identification of which markets required additional investment vs. optimisation
  • Reduced time from launch to first actionable insight from weeks to days
  • Framework influenced multi-million dollar investment allocation decisions

Lessons Learned

  • Standardisation enables speed — without templates, every launch reinvents the wheel
  • New markets need different metrics than mature ones; applying mature benchmarks too early creates false alarms
  • Cultural differences between UK and US teams require deliberate alignment on definitions and expectations
  • Regulatory constraints are data problems, not just legal problems — encode them in your models
