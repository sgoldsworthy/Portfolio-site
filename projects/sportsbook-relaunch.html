Sportsbook Relaunch

Led the analytics strategy for a major sportsbook product relaunch, combining cohort analysis, A/B testing, and commercial modelling to measure and drive growth.

SQL Python Snowflake Power BI Cohort Analysis A/B Testing

Business Problem

The sportsbook product was underperforming against market benchmarks. A full relaunch was planned — new UX, new pricing, new market offerings — but the business needed a rigorous analytical framework to measure success, forecast outcomes, and de-risk the investment.

Why It Was Difficult

  • Multiple simultaneous changes made attribution complex
  • No clean baseline existed due to ongoing promotions and seasonal variation
  • Stakeholders wanted forecasts before the product shipped — with quantified uncertainty
  • Commercial pressure to demonstrate ROI quickly

My Role

I owned the analytics strategy for the relaunch: experiment design, baseline modelling, forecasting, and ongoing measurement. I worked directly with the Head of UK Sports Commercial to frame success metrics and quantify the business case.

Approach

Experiment Design

  • Designed a phased rollout strategy with control groups
  • Defined primary and secondary success metrics
  • Built power calculations to determine sample sizes and test duration

Statistical Forecasting

  • Developed counterfactual models to estimate what would have happened without the relaunch
  • Applied time-series decomposition to isolate relaunch effects from seasonality and promotions
  • Quantified uncertainty with confidence intervals

Commercial Modelling

  • Built financial models linking product metrics to revenue outcomes
  • Created scenario analysis for different rollout speeds
  • Modelled cannibalisation effects across product lines

Architecture

Historical Data
      ↓
Baseline & Counterfactual Models
      ↓
┌──────────────────────────────────┐
│  A/B Test   │  Time Series Model │
└──────────────────────────────────┘
      ↓
Causal Impact Estimation
      ↓
Commercial Reporting → Leadership

Technologies

  • SQL — Complex analytical queries, cohort construction
  • Python — Statistical modelling, forecasting, causal inference
  • Snowflake — Data platform and computation
  • Statistical Methods — Difference-in-differences, synthetic control, Bayesian structural time series

Outcome

  • 30% sportsbook revenue growth attributed to the relaunch
  • Clear causal evidence separated relaunch effects from market trends
  • Forecasting framework reused across subsequent product launches
  • Approach became the template for all major product changes

Lessons Learned

  • Invest heavily in baseline measurement before any change — you can't prove impact without a clean counterfactual
  • Stakeholders respond better to scenarios with uncertainty ranges than point estimates
  • Speed matters: daily reporting during rollout caught issues early
  • The hardest conversations are about what you can't measure, not what you can
