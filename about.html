About

I'm an Analytics Manager specialising in commercial analytics, product analytics, and AI-driven decision intelligence. I build data products that help executives make better decisions faster.

My focus is on the intersection of governed data, modern analytics engineering, and AI — turning complex business questions into self-service products that scale beyond individual analysts.

How I Think

Measure before optimising

You cannot improve what you cannot quantify. Establish baselines before making changes.

Govern metrics

A single source of truth prevents more problems than any dashboard solves.

Products over dashboards

Analytics should be a product experience, not a reporting function.

Commercial first

Every analysis should connect to a business decision. Data without context is noise.

Automate repetitive work

If an analyst does the same thing twice, it should be automated by the third time.

AI augments analysts

AI should remove the tedious, not replace the thoughtful. The best outcomes combine human judgement with machine speed.
